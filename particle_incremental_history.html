The history of Particle Incremental

As you may or may not know,

Particle Incremental

is a game that I am currently working on. It is my own game.


So, here's the history of Particle incremental.

I just found out that the story starts earlier than I thought. So I decided to make a javascript game on the 1st of April, 2021. I made some progress, but then while trying to figure out how to implement saving, I ended up giving up on the 24th of April (2021). And then the real story starts. One day, a young XnoobSpeakable started learning python. Later, he decided to make his own game in python (which I just learned was inspired by the older javascript game). So, on the 5th of September, 2021 he decided to start. But, a bit later, after some encouragement from a discord server, he decided to turn it into a browser game by remaking it in javascript. He did a ton of planning on how the game was going to look and decided to completely change it from how the earlier python version (and even earlier javascript version) looked and played. After all this planning (and help from Cosmic47, tysm man), he decided to start. He also decided to call it Particle Incremental. This is when the really real truly true story starts. It is now the 13th of November, 2021. XnoobSpeakable proceeded to work on the game, but then. Guess what? He got stuck on saving and ended up giving up on the 3rd of December (2021). But, he remembered that he promised to himself that he'll never give up. He knew he would come back, but when? It is now the 2nd of April 2022 and he's back. He decided to almost completely rewrite the game's code and then continued working on it. On the 15th of April (2022) he released beta 1.0.0 and declared it "the first actually playable version of the game" in another discord server. And he's been actively developing it ever since. It is now the 18th of June 2022 and I'll stop referring to myself in 3rd person now. Anyway, the latest version as of today (June 18th) is beta 1.20.2. Also, I have been working on a code update for a very long time and it is the hardest thing I think I've ever done. When I finally finish it I'll release it as beta 1.21.0. It's been almost a month since Particle Incremental has had an update. Beta 1.20.2 was released on the 25th of May (2022). Anyway, I will keep my promise and not give up. I will also update this page when beta 1.21.0 is released. Bye for now!
